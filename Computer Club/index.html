About Join Now
Skilled Mentors

"Learn from the best minds in campus."

Our club is powered by experienced mentors and seniors who guide members in coding, design, and technology through hands-on training and peer collaboration.

Tech Workshops

“Stay ahead with practical learning.”

We organize coding bootcamps, design sessions, and workshops on trending tech like Web Dev, Python, AI, and more—accessible to all members from beginner to advanced.

Project Showcases

“Build from home, shine on campus.”

Members are encouraged to work on personal or team-based tech projects. Get featured in our project gallery and participate in intra-club competitions to win recognition.

Digital Resource Hub

“Learn anytime, anywhere.”

Access a growing collection of eBooks, tutorials, and past contest materials curated by club members to support your self-paced learning and development.

NDUBCC

Objective

NDUBCC was officially inaugurated at 12.45 pm on March 6, 2017. The inauguration also included a prize giving ceremony for the winners from a programming contest held on the same day.

Since its inception, the club has already organized a workshop on competitive programming with the association of SWAP (Student Welfare Association and Platform) and sent 3 teams to BUET RoboTutor v3.0. NDUBCC currently has approximately 80 members and is rapidly growing.
